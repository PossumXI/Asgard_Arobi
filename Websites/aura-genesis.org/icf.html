Intelligence Consule Federation

The elite council of experts maintaining the integrity and security of APEX-OS-LQ and the Arobi Network through Proof of Intelligence consensus.

ICF Overview

The Intelligence Consule Federation (ICF) represents the pinnacle of the Intelligencer Program, bringing together the brightest minds in technology, security, and blockchain to govern and protect our decentralized ecosystem.

Through our innovative Proof of Intelligence (PoI) consensus mechanism, we ensure that critical decisions about network security and protocol updates are made by those with proven expertise and substantial stake in the ecosystem's success.

Joining the ICF

Minimum Requirements

  • Significant AURA token holdings (top 1% of holders)
  • Verified expertise in relevant technical domain
  • Proven track record of contributions to the ecosystem
  • Nomination by existing ICF members

Application Process

Interested candidates should engage actively in the community, demonstrate technical expertise through contributions, and build relationships with current ICF members who can provide nominations.
